Instructions

The program objectives are to develop safe and nurturing environment for children and adolescent in Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda and Ethiopia through grant making and technical capacity support to community organizations and National NGO’s.

The main working strategy of CRVPF is providing grants and capacity building support to organizations in cluster partnership in implementing in one geographic area and place based cluster.

Further the cluster partnership provided a six month planning and learning grant to

  • To give cluster partnership time and space to know each other strategies, the cluster member organizations directors identify their synergies and how they can work together on the long term supportive environments.
  • To encourage cluster partners to identify a project area for collaborative program development.
  • To give time and space to listen to potential stakeholders such as children, youth, adolescent girls, families, communities and schools.
  • To encourage cluster partners to conduct situational analysis to identify children, adolescent girls, families and communities and school situations.

It’s expected that during the planning and learning grant for six months period partners may consider the following activities and outcomes.

1. Building a very strong relationship among cluster partner members
  • Organizing frequent cluster partners meetings including board members, executive and staff
  • Learning from cluster members (what others do)
  • Sign a clear MOU that shows roles and responsibility of members working towards the same goal of power building for girls.
2. Identifying Project area
  • Mapping Resources/Assets
  • Conducting situational analysis on violence against children and adolescents.
  • Conducting Community entry/ introduction of the program conversation with stakeholders or key influences in the communities.
3. CRVPF will assess and learn the capacity of the organization more especially on finance, governance and program managements.

Therefore based on the above guide, as cluster, we request you to develop and submit a six months concept on the planning and learning grant.
